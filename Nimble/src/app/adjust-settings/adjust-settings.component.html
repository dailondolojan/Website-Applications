Adjust Basic Settings

Learn how to change the brightness and volume on your devices.

Desktop

Adjust Volume

  1. Locate your volume keys on your keyboard
  2. Press the left button to lower the volume
  3. Press the right button to raise the volume
  4. If there are three buttons for volume, the third button is "mute"

Adjust Brightness

  1. Locate the brightness keys on your keyboard
  2. Press the left button to lower the brightness
  3. Press the right button to raise the brightness


Mobile Devices

Adjust Volume

  1. Locate your volume keys on the side of your device
  2. Press the higher button to raise the volume
  3. Press the lower button to lower the volume

Adjust Brightness

  1. Locate the settings icon
  2. Look for the display settings in the menu
  3. Drag the slider to the right to raise the volume
  4. Drag the slider to the left to lower the volume
  5. You can toggle auto-brightness to change the brightness automatically
  6. Shortcut: you can also adjust the volume by swiping from the top or bottom of the screen (depends on your device) to access a quick menu
