Nimble Help

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nimble?

Nimble is a web application designed to teach essential skills to help you feel comfortable and confident using their devices.

What does Nimble teach?

Nimble teaches general skills that are not device specific such as:

  • Basic terminology
  • Common icons
  • How to use apps on mobile devices
  • Gestures for touchscreen devices
  • More!
How should Nimble be used?

Nimble is designed as a tool to help teach navigation skills, not as a standalone teacher. We recommend using Nimble with a human helper and practicing the skills you learn on your own devices.

How does the matching game work?

There are 4 sets with 10 icons each. Click on an icon from the left column (it will become outlined in blue), then click on the corresponding label from the column on the right.
